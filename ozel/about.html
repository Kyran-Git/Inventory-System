    Ozel Chempaka offers many types of brooches to choose from. Mohd Hafis Bin Che Abdul Rahman and Nur Hafiza Binti Zamri started their business from a humble abode that is their home.

    As time passed, they now have their own shop at Kampung Wakaf Beruas, Kuala Terengganu.

    The company has six employees, four of the employees are full-time workers and the other two are part-timers. Ozel Cempaka is open every day from 9AM to 6PM.
